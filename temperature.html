Temperature v. Latitude (January 5, 2020)





This graph plots percent temperature vs. latitude for 612 random cities. The more northerly (positive) the latitude, the colder the temperatures. The more southerly (negative) the latitude, the warmer the temperatures. This makes sense given it is winter in the northern hemisphere and summer in the southern hemisphere on 1/5/2020.
